Beef Tapa Recipe:

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 pounds beef sirloin
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 3 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon garlic, finely minced
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil

Instructions:

1. Each piece of beef should be pounded to a quarter-inch thickness using a mallet or any other rough implement.

2. Put the beef in a basin and add the baking soda on top. Blend until well combined.

3. Pour in lemon juice and stir a few more times. Add the remaining ingredients after that.

4. Put plastic wrap over the bowl and let the meat marinade for the entire night in the refrigerator.

5. Heat a tablespoon of oil in a big skillet or pan. After 3 to 5 minutes of frying each side, the meat should be browned and the juices absorbed. Add extra oil as necessary.

6. While still hot, serve over garlic fried rice and a sunny-side up egg.
