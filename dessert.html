Halo-Halo Recipe:

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup sweetened red beans
  • 1/4 cup sweetened white beans
  • 4 cups shaved ice, from about 6 cups of ice cubes
  • 1 large banana, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup, drained jackfruit canned, sliced
  • 1/4 cup macapuno strips, drained
  • 1/4 cup nata de coco, drained
  • 1/4 cup red palm seeds, drained
  • 1/4 cup green palm seeds, drained
  • 1 cup evaporated milk
  • 1/2 cup cap'n crunch with crunch berries cereal
  • 2 scoops ube ice cream, store-bought or homemade

Instructions:

1. Assemble two tall parfait glasses and the ingredients.

2. In each glass, place half of the red and white beans that have been sweetened in the bottom.

3. In a food processor, blender, or ice shaver, process half of the ice cubes until they resemble snow. Next, cover the beans with roughly half a cup of the shaved ice.

4. Top with another half cup of shaved ice and add some slices of jackfruit and banana. Add the nata de coco and the macapuno strips.

5. Add another half cup of shaved ice on top of the remaining half of the ice cubes after processing them.

6. Stir in the green and red palm seeds. After that, drizzle each glass with the last half cup of shaved ice.

Put the green and red palm seeds in. The last half cup of shaved ice should then be added to each glass, just enough to cover it.

Sprinkle Cap'n Crunch cereal on top and start eating right away! Remember to use a large spoon to halo-halo it!
